What does it mean
    梅
  • Meaning: Prunus mume(plum), surname
  • Original meaning: Phoebe zhennan
    灵（靈）
  • Meaning: spirit, soul
  • Original meaning: dancing witch
    捷
  • Meaning: success, quickly
  • Original meaning: war trophies
Origins

The name comes from the idiom 人杰地灵 which is used to describe Jiang-xi Province where both of my parents are born. 杰 is substituted with 捷 that sounds the same (jié).

物华天宝，龙光射牛斗之墟；人杰地灵，徐孺下陈蕃之榻
—— 王勃 《滕王阁序》
物華天寶，龍光射牛斗之墟；人傑地靈，徐孺下陳蕃之榻。
—— 王勃 《滕王閣序》
There are elites in its people, since the land is blessed.
—— Wang Bo's Teng Wang Ge Xu
